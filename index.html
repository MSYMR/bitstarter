FAQs

What does "jelly shot" mean?
A "jelly shot" is basically a shot of your favorite elixir set in a clear gelatin.
What does gourmet really mean?
Webster dictionary: "a connoisseur of food and drink". Oxford dictionary: "a connoisseur of good food; a person with a discerning palate". Basically, "gourmet" equates to a taste for flavorful foods.
Does a "jelly shot" have to contain alcohol?
No. That would be like a "virgin" version of any alcoholic drink--great for any child age 2 to 92!
